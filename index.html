I'm a Johnny Taylor

And quite simply, I'm a disabled web artist searching for a little accessible value in the time I dedicate to this medium.

Toward that end, I begin to accomplish this by blogging both the personal and the technical, helping to build solutions for a more inclusive internet by volunteering my efforts with the Fluid Project, and jabbering on Twitter.

All while looking to rock life a bit harder than that of the day before — my promise to you.
